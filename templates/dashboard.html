Delivery Delay: A Logistical Challenge

In logistics and supply chain management, on-time delivery is critical. Despite efforts, delivery delays are common and can negatively affect both businesses and customers. Understanding the causes, consequences, and solutions is key to improving performance.

Impacts of Delivery Delays

  • Customer dissatisfaction and potential loss of future sales.
  • Operational disruptions like inventory and production issues.
  • Increased costs due to overtime, express shipping, or penalties.
  • Reputation damage that undermines brand trust.
  • Lost sales from cart abandonment and missed opportunities.
